### Cheat Sheet Requirements

**Structure:**
- Sections organized by topic (not question number)
- H1 headers for major topics, H2 for subtopics
- Warning callouts (⚠) in red for traps and misconceptions

**Content:**
- Two-column tables: term/concept → answer/explanation
- Yellow formula boxes for any math, with worked examples
- At the end: a Fill-in-the-blank section + Rapid-fire T/F table
- Prioritize high point-value questions

**Document settings:**
- US Letter, 0.75" margins, Arial font
- Dark blue headers (#0B3D6B), medium blue subheaders (#1565A7)
- Red warning text (#B7472A), yellow formula boxes (#FFF3CD)
